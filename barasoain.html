"MUSULEO NG REPUBLIKA NG 1899"

To arrange a visit or book a tour with the Museum of “Musuleo ng Republika ng 1899”, we offer convenient options tailored to suit different group sizes. For groups consisting of more than 20 individuals, please reach out directly via email at mr1899@nhcp.gov.ph. Alternatively, you can contact the physical museum by phone at (044) 794 1674 to secure your reservation. If you're planning a visit with a smaller group of less than 20 people, there's no need for advance booking. You're welcome to simply walk in during the operating hours.
